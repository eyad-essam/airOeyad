About Us

Qatar Airways is the state-owned flag carrier of Qatar. The airline operates a hub-and-spoke network, linking over 150 international destinations across Africa, Asia, Europe, The Americas, and Oceania from its base at Hamad International Airport in Doha. Qatar Airways Group employs over 43,000 people. The airline has been a member of the Oneworld alliance since October 2013, the first Persian Gulf carrier to sign with one of the three airline alliances.

Known for its exceptional service and luxurious travel experience, Qatar Airways has consistently been ranked among the top airlines globally. It has received numerous awards for its cabin product, in-flight entertainment, and overall passenger satisfaction. The airline's commitment to innovation and customer comfort is reflected in its modern fleet and state-of-the-art facilities. Qatar Airways continues to expand its network and enhance its services, solidifying its position as a leader in the aviation industry.
